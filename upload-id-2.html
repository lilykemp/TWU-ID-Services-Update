ID Services

Upload a photo for your Pioneer Card

Submit your own photo for review to be used as your official ID picture! This option is still in beta, so please be patient. Here are some guidelines to consider when choosing your picture:

  • Make sure the file format is PNG, JPG, or JPEG.
  • The background of your photo should be blank and white, with no other objects.
  • Your photo should include only your shoulders up to the top of your head.
  • Do not wear a hat, sunglasses, or any other item that obstructs your face.
  • Be sure to smile!
